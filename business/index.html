The Brick Society

Real Growth through Real Connection

After 9 years teaching Design and Development, it's clear the game has changed. Unlike other education programs that promise instant skills through async videos, and mass standardization, Brick Society connects designers in live feedback sessions, ongoing conversation, and self-directed group learning. It's Montessori for working designers.

In addition to resources, tools, and online discussions, Members can attend multiple live lessons and workshops a week - before, durring, or after work. You won't notice they're gone, but you will notice the skills they bring back week after week.

Nick Anderson (Founder)

Join the Club, or Lead the Pack with Personalized Mentorship

For a lot of designers, just being around folks with more experience is enough for them to improve substantially. A Member plan is a great place for lone designers or teams of entry-level talent that don't have more senior staff to learn under.

Then there's designers that are off to a great start, but missed some key fundementals along the way. Maybe they've got impressive visual design skills, but can't seem to hold it together when presenting in front of a client. Maybe you find yourself wishing their scoping estimates were as accurate as their research. The Mentee plan guides the creation of strategic goal-setting and customized steps to get them to where they need to be. Hard skills, soft skills, and everything in between.
